macOS Audio Permissions

Enable audio recording for Cheating Daddy.

1

Open System Settings

Open System Settings and go to Privacy & Security.
2

Go to Screen and System Audio Recording

Find and click on "Screen and System Audio Recording".
3

Add Cheating Daddy

Click the "+" button to add the app.
4

Select the App

Browse to Cheating Daddy and click Open.
5

Enable Permissions

Make sure the toggle is enabled.
