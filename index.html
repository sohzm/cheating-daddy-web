FAQ

Is it free?

Yes. 100% free and open source. No premium tier, no hidden fees.

Is it automatic?

Yes. Generates responses from screen and audio automatically. Use Ctrl+Enter for manual input.

Is it really invisible?

Yes. Doesn't appear in screen recordings, screen shares, or any capture software.

What platforms?

Windows and macOS. Linux coming soon.

How does it work?

Captures your screen and audio, sends to Google Gemini API, returns answers. Requires your own API key.

Is the API key free?

Google offers a free tier for Gemini API. That's enough for most interviews.

Can I customize responses?

Yes. Add your resume, job description, company info, or any context to personalize answers.
